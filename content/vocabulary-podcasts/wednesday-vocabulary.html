Wednesday Vocabulary Podcast: Health & Wellbeing

TEMA DEL DÍA

Vocabulario relacionado con la salud, el bienestar y el cuerpo.

Nivel: B1 | Duración estimada: 10-15 minutos

Podcast Script

INTRO:

"Hello and welcome to Wednesday Vocabulary! Today we're focusing on health and wellbeing. This vocabulary is essential for visiting the doctor, talking about how you feel, and discussing healthy habits. Let's begin!"

SECTION 1: Common Illnesses and Symptoms

"First, let's learn how to describe health problems. Listen and repeat."

headache /ˈhedeɪk/ dolor de cabeza

"I've had a terrible headache all day."

sore throat /sɔː θrəʊt/ dolor de garganta

"I can't talk much because I have a sore throat."

food poisoning /fuːd ˈpɔɪzənɪŋ/ intoxicación alimentaria

"I think I got food poisoning from the restaurant."

fever / temperature /ˈfiːvə/ fiebre

"She has a high fever and needs to rest."

allergy /ˈælədʒi/ alergia

"I have an allergy to peanuts."

SECTION 2: At the Doctor's

"Now let's learn vocabulary for a doctor's visit."

appointment /əˈpɔɪntmənt/ cita

"I'd like to make an appointment with the doctor."

prescription /prɪˈskrɪpʃən/ receta

"The doctor gave me a prescription for antibiotics."

examination /ɪɡˌzæmɪˈneɪʃən/ examen médico

"The doctor did a thorough examination."

side effects /saɪd ɪˈfekts/ efectos secundarios

"This medicine may cause drowsiness as a side effect."

symptoms /ˈsɪmptəmz/ síntomas

"What are your main symptoms?"

SECTION 3: Healthy Habits

"Let's learn vocabulary about staying healthy."

balanced diet /ˈbælənst ˈdaɪət/ dieta equilibrada

"A balanced diet includes fruits, vegetables, and protein."

work out /wɜːk aʊt/ hacer ejercicio

"I try to work out at the gym three times a week."

get enough sleep /ɡet ɪˈnʌf sliːp/ dormir lo suficiente

"It's important to get enough sleep every night."

quit smoking /kwɪt ˈsməʊkɪŋ/ dejar de fumar

"My New Year resolution is to quit smoking."

stress management /stres ˈmænɪdʒmənt/ gestión del estrés

"Meditation helps with stress management."

SECTION 4: Mental Health

"Finally, vocabulary related to mental wellbeing."

anxiety /æŋˈzaɪəti/ ansiedad

"She suffers from anxiety before exams."

depression /dɪˈpreʃən/ depresión

"Depression is a serious condition that needs treatment."

wellbeing /welˈbiːɪŋ/ bienestar

"Exercise is good for your physical and mental wellbeing."

mindfulness /ˈmaɪndfʊlnəs/ atención plena

"I practice mindfulness meditation every morning."

self-care /self keə/ autocuidado

"Self-care includes rest, hobbies, and spending time with friends."

OUTRO:

"Well done! Health vocabulary is really important for everyday life. Remember to take care of your health - both physical and mental. See you tomorrow for Thursday's vocabulary podcast. Stay healthy and goodbye!"

Practice Exercises

EJERCICIO 1: Complete the sentences
  1. I need to make an __________ with my doctor for next week.
  2. The doctor wrote me a __________ for some medicine.
  3. I've been trying to eat a more __________ __________ with lots of vegetables.
  4. This medication may have some __________ __________ like drowsiness.
  5. I practice __________ to help reduce my stress.
ANSWERS
  1. appointment
  2. prescription
  3. balanced diet
  4. side effects
  5. mindfulness
SPEAKING PRACTICE

Use today's vocabulary to answer these questions:

  1. What do you do to stay healthy?
  2. Describe the last time you were ill. What symptoms did you have?
  3. What advice would you give someone who wants to improve their wellbeing?

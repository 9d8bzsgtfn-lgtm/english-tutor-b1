Friday Vocabulary Podcast: Entertainment & Leisure

TEMA DEL DÍA

Vocabulario relacionado con entretenimiento, ocio y tiempo libre.

Nivel: B1 | Duración estimada: 10-15 minutos

Podcast Script

INTRO:

"Hello and welcome to Friday Vocabulary! It's the end of the week, so let's learn vocabulary about entertainment and leisure. This is perfect vocabulary for talking about your hobbies, favourite shows, and weekend plans. Let's start!"

SECTION 1: Films and Cinema

"First, let's learn vocabulary about films and cinema. Listen and repeat."

blockbuster /ˈblɒkbʌstə/ superproducción, éxito de taquilla

"The new Marvel film is a real blockbuster."

sequel /ˈsiːkwəl/ secuela

"They're making a sequel to that popular film."

plot /plɒt/ trama, argumento

"The plot was confusing but the acting was great."

subtitles /ˈsʌbtaɪtlz/ subtítulos

"I prefer watching films with English subtitles."

spoiler /ˈspɔɪlə/ spoiler

"Don't tell me the ending - no spoilers please!"

SECTION 2: TV Shows and Streaming

"Now let's learn vocabulary for TV and streaming."

binge-watch /bɪndʒ wɒtʃ/ ver de un tirón, maratón

"I binge-watched the whole series in one weekend."

episode /ˈepɪsəʊd/ episodio, capítulo

"The latest episode had a shocking ending."

season / series /ˈsiːzən/ temporada

"The new season starts next month."

documentary /ˌdɒkjuˈmentəri/ documental

"I watched a fascinating documentary about nature."

sitcom /ˈsɪtkɒm/ comedia de situación

"Friends is one of the most popular sitcoms ever."

SECTION 3: Music

"Let's learn music vocabulary."

gig / concert /ɡɪɡ/ concierto

"We're going to a gig at the stadium tonight."

lyrics /ˈlɪrɪks/ letra (de canción)

"I love the lyrics of this song."

catchy /ˈkætʃi/ pegadizo

"That song is so catchy - I can't stop singing it."

playlist /ˈpleɪlɪst/ lista de reproducción

"I made a playlist for my workout."

live performance /laɪv pəˈfɔːməns/ actuación en directo

"Her live performances are incredible."

SECTION 4: Hobbies and Free Time

"Finally, vocabulary for hobbies and leisure activities."

take up (a hobby) /teɪk ʌp/ empezar (un hobby)

"I want to take up photography this year."

pastime /ˈpɑːstaɪm/ pasatiempo

"Reading is my favourite pastime."

relaxing /rɪˈlæksɪŋ/ relajante

"Gardening is very relaxing for me."

challenging /ˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋ/ desafiante

"Rock climbing is challenging but exciting."

rewarding /rɪˈwɔːdɪŋ/ gratificante

"Volunteering is a very rewarding hobby."

OUTRO:

"Fantastic! Now you have plenty of vocabulary to talk about what you like to do in your free time. Enjoy your weekend and see you on Saturday for more vocabulary! Have fun and goodbye!"

Practice Exercises

EJERCICIO 1: Complete the sentences
  1. Don't tell me what happens in the film - no __________ please!
  2. I __________ the whole series in one weekend.
  3. That song is so __________ - I've been singing it all day.
  4. I decided to __________ a new hobby: painting.
  5. I watched a great __________ about climate change.
ANSWERS
  1. spoilers
  2. binge-watched
  3. catchy
  4. take up
  5. documentary
SPEAKING PRACTICE

Use today's vocabulary to answer these questions:

  1. What's your favourite film or TV series? Describe it without spoilers!
  2. Have you ever binge-watched something? What was it?
  3. What hobby would you like to take up? Why?

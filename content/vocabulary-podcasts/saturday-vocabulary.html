Saturday Vocabulary Podcast: Food & Dining

TEMA DEL DÍA

Vocabulario relacionado con comida, cocina y restaurantes.

Nivel: B1 | Duración estimada: 10-15 minutos

Podcast Script

INTRO:

"Hello and welcome to Saturday Vocabulary! It's the weekend, and today we're learning vocabulary about food and dining. This is perfect for ordering at restaurants, discussing recipes, and talking about your favourite dishes. Let's get cooking!"

SECTION 1: Cooking Methods

"First, let's learn different ways to cook food. Listen and repeat."

roast /rəʊst/ asar (al horno)

"We roast chicken every Sunday."

grill /ɡrɪl/ asar a la parrilla

"I prefer to grill fish rather than fry it."

steam /stiːm/ cocinar al vapor

"Steaming vegetables keeps more nutrients."

stir-fry /stɜː fraɪ/ saltear

"I like to stir-fry vegetables with garlic and soy sauce."

simmer /ˈsɪmə/ cocer a fuego lento

"Let the sauce simmer for 20 minutes."

SECTION 2: Describing Food

"Now let's learn adjectives to describe food."

delicious /dɪˈlɪʃəs/ delicioso

"This pasta is absolutely delicious!"

bland /blænd/ soso, insípido

"The soup was a bit bland - it needed more seasoning."

spicy /ˈspaɪsi/ picante

"I love spicy food, especially Indian curry."

crispy /ˈkrɪspi/ crujiente

"I like my bacon extra crispy."

tender /ˈtendə/ tierno

"The meat was so tender it melted in my mouth."

SECTION 3: At the Restaurant

"Let's learn vocabulary for eating out."

reservation /ˌrezəˈveɪʃən/ reserva

"I'd like to make a reservation for 8 o'clock."

starter / appetizer /ˈstɑːtə/ entrante

"Would you like a starter before your main course?"

main course /meɪn kɔːs/ plato principal

"For the main course, I'll have the steak."

side dish /saɪd dɪʃ/ acompañamiento

"What side dishes do you have?"

bill / check /bɪl/ cuenta

"Could we have the bill, please?"

SECTION 4: Diets and Preferences

"Finally, vocabulary for dietary requirements."

vegetarian /ˌvedʒɪˈteəriən/ vegetariano

"I've been vegetarian for five years."

vegan /ˈviːɡən/ vegano

"Do you have any vegan options?"

gluten-free /ˈɡluːtən friː/ sin gluten

"I need to eat gluten-free because of my allergy."

organic /ɔːˈɡænɪk/ orgánico, ecológico

"I prefer to buy organic vegetables."

portion /ˈpɔːʃən/ porción, ración

"The portions at this restaurant are huge!"

OUTRO:

"Wonderful! Now you're ready to talk about food in English. Why not practice by describing what you're having for dinner tonight? See you tomorrow for Sunday's vocabulary podcast. Bon appétit and goodbye!"

Practice Exercises

EJERCICIO 1: Choose the correct word
  1. The chicken was very __________ - it practically fell off the bone. (crispy / tender / bland)
  2. I'd like to make a __________ for Saturday evening. (bill / portion / reservation)
  3. This curry is very __________! I need some water. (tender / spicy / bland)
  4. Let the soup __________ for an hour to develop the flavour. (grill / simmer / steam)
  5. For a healthy option, I prefer to __________ my vegetables. (fry / steam / roast)
ANSWERS
  1. tender
  2. reservation
  3. spicy
  4. simmer
  5. steam
SPEAKING PRACTICE

Use today's vocabulary to answer these questions:

  1. What's your favourite type of food? How would you describe it?
  2. Do you prefer eating at home or at restaurants? Why?
  3. Have you ever had a memorable meal? Describe it.

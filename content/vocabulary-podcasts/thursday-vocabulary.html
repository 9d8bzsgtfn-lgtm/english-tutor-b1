Thursday Vocabulary Podcast: Technology & Digital Life

TEMA DEL DÍA

Vocabulario relacionado con tecnología, internet y la vida digital.

Nivel: B1 | Duración estimada: 10-15 minutos

Podcast Script

INTRO:

"Hello and welcome to Thursday Vocabulary! Today we're going to learn vocabulary about technology and digital life. Technology is everywhere, so this vocabulary will be very useful for your daily conversations. Let's dive in!"

SECTION 1: Devices and Gadgets

"First, let's learn about common devices. Listen and repeat."

smartphone /ˈsmɑːtfəʊn/ teléfono inteligente

"I can't imagine life without my smartphone."

tablet /ˈtæblət/ tableta

"I use my tablet for reading e-books."

laptop /ˈlæptɒp/ portátil

"I take my laptop everywhere for work."

wearable /ˈweərəbəl/ dispositivo vestible

"My smartwatch is a wearable that tracks my fitness."

charger /ˈtʃɑːdʒə/ cargador

"I forgot my phone charger at home."

SECTION 2: Internet and Online Activities

"Now let's learn vocabulary for online activities."

browse /braʊz/ navegar

"I spend hours browsing the internet."

download /ˈdaʊnləʊd/ descargar

"I need to download the new app update."

upload /ʌpˈləʊd/ subir

"I uploaded my holiday photos to social media."

streaming /ˈstriːmɪŋ/ transmisión en directo

"I prefer streaming films to going to the cinema."

Wi-Fi /ˈwaɪfaɪ/ Wi-Fi

"Is there free Wi-Fi in this café?"

SECTION 3: Social Media

"Let's learn vocabulary about social media."

post /pəʊst/ publicación / publicar

"Did you see her latest post on Instagram?"

follower /ˈfɒləʊə/ seguidor

"She has thousands of followers on Twitter."

go viral /ɡəʊ ˈvaɪrəl/ hacerse viral

"His video went viral overnight."

hashtag /ˈhæʃtæɡ/ hashtag, etiqueta

"Use the hashtag to find related posts."

notification /ˌnəʊtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ notificación

"I turned off my notifications to concentrate."

SECTION 4: Problems and Solutions

"Finally, vocabulary for tech problems."

crash /kræʃ/ bloquearse, fallar

"My computer crashed and I lost all my work."

bug /bʌɡ/ error, bug

"There's a bug in the software that needs fixing."

backup /ˈbækʌp/ copia de seguridad

"Always make a backup of important files."

password /ˈpɑːswɜːd/ contraseña

"You should use a strong password with numbers and symbols."

update /ˈʌpdeɪt/ actualización / actualizar

"Don't forget to update your antivirus software."

OUTRO:

"Brilliant! Technology vocabulary is essential in today's world. Practice using these words when you talk about your digital life. See you tomorrow for Friday's vocabulary podcast. Stay connected and goodbye!"

Practice Exercises

EJERCICIO 1: Fill in the blanks
  1. I need to __________ the latest version of the app.
  2. Can you give me the Wi-Fi __________? I need to connect.
  3. My phone __________ and I had to restart it.
  4. She has over a million __________ on Instagram.
  5. Remember to __________ your files regularly.
ANSWERS
  1. download
  2. password
  3. crashed
  4. followers
  5. backup / back up
SPEAKING PRACTICE

Use today's vocabulary to answer these questions:

  1. What devices do you use every day?
  2. How much time do you spend on social media?
  3. Have you ever had a problem with technology? What happened?

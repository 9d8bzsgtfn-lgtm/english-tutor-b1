Sunday Vocabulary Podcast: Environment & Nature

TEMA DEL DÍA

Vocabulario relacionado con el medio ambiente, la naturaleza y la sostenibilidad.

Nivel: B1 | Duración estimada: 10-15 minutos

Podcast Script

INTRO:

"Hello and welcome to Sunday Vocabulary! To end the week, we're going to learn vocabulary about the environment and nature. This is an important topic for discussions, essays, and everyday conversations. Let's explore!"

SECTION 1: Environmental Problems

"First, let's learn vocabulary about environmental issues. Listen and repeat."

climate change /ˈklaɪmət tʃeɪndʒ/ cambio climático

"Climate change is one of the biggest challenges we face."

global warming /ˈɡləʊbəl ˈwɔːmɪŋ/ calentamiento global

"Global warming is causing glaciers to melt."

pollution /pəˈluːʃən/ contaminación

"Air pollution is a serious problem in big cities."

deforestation /diːˌfɒrɪˈsteɪʃən/ deforestación

"Deforestation is destroying the Amazon rainforest."

endangered species /ɪnˈdeɪndʒəd ˈspiːʃiːz/ especies en peligro de extinción

"Many endangered species need protection."

SECTION 2: Sustainability Solutions

"Now let's learn vocabulary about protecting the environment."

recycle /riːˈsaɪkəl/ reciclar

"We recycle paper, plastic, and glass at home."

renewable energy /rɪˈnjuːəbəl ˈenədʒi/ energía renovable

"Solar and wind are types of renewable energy."

sustainable /səˈsteɪnəbəl/ sostenible

"We need to find more sustainable ways to live."

carbon footprint /ˈkɑːbən ˈfʊtprɪnt/ huella de carbono

"I'm trying to reduce my carbon footprint."

eco-friendly /ˈiːkəʊ frendli/ ecológico

"I try to buy eco-friendly products."

SECTION 3: Nature and Wildlife

"Let's learn vocabulary about nature."

wildlife /ˈwaɪldlaɪf/ fauna, vida silvestre

"The park is home to amazing wildlife."

habitat /ˈhæbɪtæt/ hábitat

"Animals lose their habitat when forests are cut down."

conservation /ˌkɒnsəˈveɪʃən/ conservación

"Conservation efforts are helping to save the whales."

ecosystem /ˈiːkəʊsɪstəm/ ecosistema

"Coral reefs are delicate ecosystems."

biodiversity /ˌbaɪəʊdaɪˈvɜːsɪti/ biodiversidad

"The rainforest has incredible biodiversity."

SECTION 4: Taking Action

"Finally, vocabulary for environmental actions."

reduce /rɪˈdjuːs/ reducir

"We should reduce our use of plastic."

reuse /riːˈjuːz/ reutilizar

"I reuse shopping bags instead of buying new ones."

plant trees /plɑːnt triːz/ plantar árboles

"Many organisations plant trees to help the environment."

raise awareness /reɪz əˈweənəs/ concienciar

"We need to raise awareness about climate change."

make a difference /meɪk ə ˈdɪfərəns/ marcar la diferencia

"Every small action can make a difference."

OUTRO:

"Excellent work this week! Environmental vocabulary is very useful for discussions and written work. Remember: reduce, reuse, recycle! That's the end of our weekly vocabulary podcasts. Start again on Monday with Work & Career. Have a great week and goodbye!"

Practice Exercises

EJERCICIO 1: Match the words

Match each problem with its solution:

ProblemSolution
1. Air pollutiona. Plant trees
2. Plastic wasteb. Renewable energy
3. Deforestationc. Reduce and recycle
4. Fossil fuel used. Electric cars
ANSWERS
  1. 1-d (Air pollution → Electric cars)
  2. 2-c (Plastic waste → Reduce and recycle)
  3. 3-a (Deforestation → Plant trees)
  4. 4-b (Fossil fuel use → Renewable energy)
SPEAKING PRACTICE

Use today's vocabulary to answer these questions:

  1. What environmental problems do you think are most serious?
  2. What do you do to help the environment?
  3. What changes should governments make to protect the planet?

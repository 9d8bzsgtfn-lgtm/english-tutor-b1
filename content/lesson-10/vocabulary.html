Useful Expressions with Relative Clauses

EXPRESIONES UTILES
Expression Example Translation
the reason why That's the reason why I called you. La razon por la que...
the way (that) I don't like the way he talks. La forma en que...
the thing (that) The thing I like most is the view. Lo que mas...
what (= the thing that) What I need is a holiday. Lo que necesito es...
all (that) All I want is some peace. Todo lo que quiero...
everything (that) Everything he said was true. Todo lo que dijo...

Time Expressions with WHEN

EXPRESIONES DE TIEMPO
Expression Example
the day when I remember the day when we met.
the moment when That was the moment when I knew.
the time when There was a time when I believed that.
the year when 2020 was the year when everything changed.

Nota: Podemos omitir "when" en estas expresiones: I remember the day we met.

PRACTICA

Pregunta al tutor usando relative clauses:

  • "Can you describe a person who you admire?"
  • "What's a place where you feel relaxed?"
  • "Tell me about something that you can't live without."

Grammar: Present Simple vs Present Continuous

IMPRESCINDIBLE - MEMORIZAR

Regla fundamental

Tiempo Uso Ejemplo
Present Simple Rutinas, hábitos, hechos permanentes I work from home.
Present Continuous Acciones en progreso AHORA o temporales I'm working from home this week.
TRUCO PARA RECORDAR

SIMPLE = Siempre / Generalmente (rutina)

CONTINUOUS = Currently / Ahora mismo (temporal)

Estructura gramatical

Present Simple Present Continuous
+ I/You/We/They work
+ He/She/It works 		+ I am working
+ You/We/They are working
+ He/She/It is working
- I/You/We/They don't work
- He/She/It doesn't work 		- I am not working
- You/We/They aren't working
- He/She/It isn't working
? Do you work?
? Does he work? 		? Are you working?

Señales clave (Keywords)

KEYWORDS

Ejemplos comparados

Present Simple Present Continuous
I drink coffee.
(Rutina: siempre bebo café)		 I'm drinking coffee.
(Ahora mismo tengo un café)
She works at a bank.
(Es su trabajo fijo)		 She's working at a bank this summer.
(Temporal, solo este verano)
They live in Madrid.
(Permanente)		 They're living in Madrid for a few months.
(Temporal)
STATIVE VERBS - MUY IMPORTANTE

Verbos que NO llevan -ing (normalmente)

Estos verbos describen estados, no acciones, por eso no se usan en Continuous:

Categoría Verbos
Emociones like, love, hate, prefer, want, need
Mente know, believe, understand, remember, think*, mean
Sentidos see*, hear, smell*, taste*, feel*
Posesión have*, own, belong, possess
Otros be*, seem, appear, cost, contain

* Algunos tienen doble uso (estado vs acción).

Errores comunes a evitar

ERRORES FRECUENTES EN EXAMEN
Incorrecto Correcto Por qué
I'm liking this. I like this. "Like" es stative verb
He work every day. He works every day. 3ª persona lleva -s
I working now. I'm working now. Falta el verbo "to be"
Does she works? Does she work? Con "does" no se añade -s
I'm not believing you. I don't believe you. "Believe" es stative verb
RESUMEN "SUBE LAS ANTENAS"
  • Present Simple: rutinas, hábitos, hechos generales + keywords (always, usually, every...)
  • Present Continuous: ahora mismo, temporal + keywords (now, at the moment, this week...)
  • Stative verbs: NO llevan -ing (like, know, want, need, believe...)
  • 3ª persona singular: añade -s en Present Simple (he works, she likes)
  • Continuous: siempre con BE + -ing (am/is/are + verb-ing)

Practice: Present Simple vs Present Continuous

INSTRUCCIONES

Selecciona la respuesta correcta para cada ejercicio. Recibirás feedback inmediato.

Exercise 1: Choose the correct form

1. I usually ________ coffee in the morning.

drink
am drinking
drinks

Correct! "Usually" indicates a routine, so we use Present Simple. The subject is "I", so no -s needed.

2. Look! It ________ outside.

rains
is raining
rain

Correct! "Look!" tells us it's happening right now, so we use Present Continuous.

3. She ________ English very well.

speaks
is speaking
speak

Correct! This is a permanent ability/skill, so we use Present Simple. "She" needs -s.

4. I ________ this book. It's very interesting.

am liking
like
likes

Correct! "Like" is a stative verb. Stative verbs don't use -ing form.

5. What ________ at the moment?

do you do
are you doing
you are doing

Correct! "At the moment" = now, so we use Present Continuous. Question form: Are + subject + -ing.

Exercise 2: Fill in the gaps

6. He ________ (work) in a bank. He's a manager.

works
is working
work

Correct! This describes his permanent job, so Present Simple. "He" = -s.

7. This week, I ________ (stay) at my parents' house.

stay
am staying
stays

Correct! "This week" indicates a temporary situation, so Present Continuous.

8. Water ________ (boil) at 100 degrees Celsius.

boils
is boiling
boil

Correct! This is a scientific fact (always true), so Present Simple.

9. Sorry, I can't talk now. I ________ (drive).

drive
am driving
drives

Correct! "Now" and "can't talk" indicate action in progress, so Present Continuous.

10. They ________ (not/understand) the question.

don't understand
aren't understanding
doesn't understand

Correct! "Understand" is a stative verb, so no -ing. "They" = don't (not doesn't).

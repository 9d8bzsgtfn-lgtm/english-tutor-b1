Practice: Passive Voice

INSTRUCCIONES

Completa con la forma pasiva correcta del verbo.

Exercise 1: Present Simple Passive

1. English ________ (speak) in many countries around the world.

is spoken
are spoken
was spoken

Correct! Present simple passive: IS + past participle (spoken). "English" is singular.

2. These cars ________ (manufacture) in Japan.

is manufactured
are manufactured
was manufactured

Correct! "These cars" is plural, so we use ARE + manufactured.

Exercise 2: Past Simple Passive

3. The Eiffel Tower ________ (build) in 1889.

is built
was built
has been built

Correct! Past simple passive (1889 = specific time in the past): WAS + built.

4. The documents ________ (steal) from the office last night.

was stolen
were stolen
are stolen

Correct! "Documents" is plural + past time (last night) = WERE stolen.

Exercise 3: Future & Present Perfect Passive

5. The results ________ (announce) tomorrow morning.

will be announced
are announced
have been announced

Correct! Future passive (tomorrow): WILL BE + announced.

6. The email ________ (send) already. Check your inbox.

was sent
has been sent
is sent

Correct! Present perfect passive (already = connection to present): HAS BEEN + sent.

Exercise 4: Modal Passive

7. This work ________ (must / complete) by Friday.

must be completed
must completed
must be complete

Correct! Modal passive: MUST + BE + past participle (completed).

8. The problem ________ (can / solve) easily.

can solved
can be solve
can be solved

Correct! Modal passive: CAN + BE + past participle (solved).

Exercise 5: Active to Passive

9. "Someone broke the window." = The window ________.

is broken
was broken
has been broken

Correct! Active "broke" (past) becomes passive "was broken". No agent needed (someone = unknown).

10. "They are repairing the road." = The road ________.

is repaired
is being repaired
was being repaired

Correct! Present continuous passive: IS BEING + repaired (action in progress now).

Practice: Past Simple vs Past Continuous

INSTRUCCIONES

Selecciona la respuesta correcta para cada ejercicio. Recibirás feedback inmediato.

Exercise 1: Choose the correct form

1. I ________ TV when the doorbell rang.

watched
was watching
watch

Correct! The doorbell "rang" (short action) interrupted "watching TV" (long action in progress). Use Past Continuous for the background action.

2. She ________ to Paris last summer.

was going
went
goes

Correct! "Last summer" indicates a completed action in the past. Use Past Simple.

3. While I ________ dinner, my friend called.

was cooking
cooked
cook

Correct! "While" introduces the longer background action, so we use Past Continuous.

4. They ________ football when it started to rain.

played
were playing
play

Correct! Playing football was in progress when the rain started (interruption).

5. What ________ at 8pm yesterday?

did you do
were you doing
you did

Correct! "At 8pm" asks about what was in progress at a specific moment, so Past Continuous.

Exercise 2: Complete with WHEN or WHILE

6. ________ I was walking home, I saw an accident.

While
When

Correct! "While" introduces the Past Continuous (longer action).

7. ________ the phone rang, I was in the shower.

While
When

Correct! "When" introduces the Past Simple (short interruption).

Exercise 3: Past Simple or Past Continuous?

8. I (meet) ________ my wife in 2010.

met
was meeting

Correct! "In 2010" indicates a completed event. Use Past Simple.

9. At midnight, everyone (sleep) ________.

slept
was sleeping

Correct! "At midnight" asks about an action in progress at a specific time.

10. She (not hear) ________ the alarm because she (listen) ________ to music.

didn't hear / was listening
wasn't hearing / listened
didn't hear / listened

Correct! "Didn't hear" is the main event (Simple). "Was listening" is the background action (Continuous).

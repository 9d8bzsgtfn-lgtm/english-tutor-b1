Vocabulary: Conditionals & Decisions

TEMA

Vocabulario relacionado con condiciones, probabilidad, planes y decisiones.

Conditional Connectors

unless
/ənˈles/
a menos que
"I won't go unless you come with me."
provided (that)
/prəˈvaɪdɪd/
siempre que, con tal de que
"You can go out provided that you finish your homework."
as long as
/æz lɒŋ æz/
siempre que, mientras
"You can borrow my car as long as you drive carefully."
in case
/ɪn keɪs/
por si (acaso)
"Take some cash in case the card machine doesn't work."
even if
/ˈiːvən ɪf/
aunque, incluso si
"I'll support you even if everyone else disagrees."
otherwise
/ˈʌðəwaɪz/
de lo contrario, si no
"Hurry up, otherwise we'll be late."

Useful Conditional Phrases

FRASES UTILES
Phrase Example
What would you do if...? What would you do if you lost your job?
If I were in your shoes... If I were in your shoes, I'd accept the offer.
It depends (on)... It depends on how much it costs.
Just in case... Take a jacket just in case it gets cold.
What if...? What if we miss the train?
Suppose/Supposing... Suppose you won the lottery, what would you buy?
PRACTICA

Practica con el tutor usando condicionales:

  • "What would you do if you could travel anywhere?"
  • "If you had a million euros, what would you buy?"
  • "What will you do if it rains this weekend?"

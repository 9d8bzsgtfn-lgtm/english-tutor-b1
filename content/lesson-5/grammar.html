Grammar: Comparatives & Superlatives

IMPRESCINDIBLE - MEMORIZAR

Regla fundamental

Tipo Uso Ejemplo
Comparative Comparar DOS cosas/personas London is bigger than Madrid.
Superlative Comparar con TODOS los demás Tokyo is the biggest city in the world.

Formación: Adjetivos cortos (1 sílaba)

ADJETIVOS CORTOS
Adjective Comparative Superlative

Regla: adjetivo + -er / the + adjetivo + -est

Estructuras comparativas

ESTRUCTURAS CLAVE
Estructura Ejemplo Significado
comparative + than She's taller than me. Comparar dos cosas
as + adj + as He's as tall as his father. Igualdad
not as + adj + as It's not as cold as yesterday. Menos que (desigualdad)
less + adj + than This is less expensive than that. Menos... que
the + superlative + in/of He's the best player in the team. El mejor de todos

Errores comunes a evitar

ERRORES FRECUENTES
Incorrecto Correcto Por qué
more better better GOOD es irregular, no usa MORE
more cheaper cheaper Adjetivo corto: solo -er
the most tall the tallest Adjetivo corto: solo -est
bigger that bigger than Comparativo + THAN (no THAT)
She's taller that me. She's taller than me. THAN, no THAT
RESUMEN "SUBE LAS ANTENAS"
  • Cortos (1 sílaba): -er / the -est
  • Largos (2+ sílabas): more / the most
  • Irregulares: good-better-best, bad-worse-worst
  • Comparativo + THAN (no THAT)
  • Superlativo + THE + in/of
  • AS...AS para igualdad

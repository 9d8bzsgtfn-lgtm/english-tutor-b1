Practice: Comparatives & Superlatives

INSTRUCCIONES

Completa con la forma correcta del adjetivo.

Exercise 1: Form the comparative

1. My car is ________ (fast) than yours.

faster
more fast
fastest

Correct! "Fast" is short (1 syllable), so we add -er.

2. This book is ________ (interesting) than that one.

interestinger
more interesting
most interesting

Correct! "Interesting" is long, so we use MORE + adjective.

3. The weather today is ________ (bad) than yesterday.

badder
more bad
worse

Correct! BAD is irregular: bad - worse - worst.

4. She's ________ (happy) now than before.

happier
more happy
happyer

Correct! Adjectives ending in -y change to -ier.

Exercise 2: Form the superlative

5. This is ________ (good) restaurant in the city.

the goodest
the most good
the best

Correct! GOOD is irregular: good - better - the best.

6. Mount Everest is ________ (high) mountain in the world.

the highest
the most high
higher

Correct! "High" is short, so THE + adjective + -EST.

7. It was ________ (expensive) hotel I've ever stayed in.

the expensivest
the most expensive
more expensive

Correct! "Expensive" is long, so THE MOST + adjective.

Exercise 3: AS...AS or comparative?

8. My brother is ________ me. We're both 1.80m.

as tall as
taller than
the tallest

Correct! They're the same height, so AS...AS (equality).

9. This exam wasn't ________ I expected.

as difficult as
more difficult than
the most difficult

Correct! NOT AS...AS = less than expected.

10. The new iPhone is much ________ the old model.

better than
as good as
the best

Correct! MUCH + comparative (much better than).

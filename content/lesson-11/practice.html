Practice: Phrasal Verbs

INSTRUCCIONES

Completa las oraciones con el phrasal verb correcto.

Exercise 1: Choose the correct phrasal verb

1. I need to ________ early tomorrow for my flight.

get on
get up
get off

Correct! "Get up" means to rise from bed. "Get on/off" is for transport.

2. Can you ________ the TV? I can't hear anything.

turn off
turn up
turn down

Correct! "Turn up" means to increase the volume. "Turn down" means decrease.

3. I'm really ________ to the summer holidays!

looking after
looking for
looking forward to

Correct! "Look forward to" means to anticipate something with pleasure.

4. She ________ her mother - they have the same eyes and smile.

takes after
looks after
takes on

Correct! "Take after" means to resemble a family member. "Look after" means to care for.

Exercise 2: Separable or inseparable?

5. Please turn ________ the music. It's too loud.

it down
down it
it to down

Correct! With pronouns, separable phrasal verbs MUST be separated: turn IT down.

6. Who will look ________ the children while we're away?

after
them after
for after

Correct! "Look after" is inseparable - the object always comes after the particle.

7. I've decided to ________ smoking.

give up
give off
give out

Correct! "Give up" means to stop doing something, especially a habit.

Exercise 3: Complete with the correct form

8. We've ________ of milk. Can you buy some?

run away
run out
run into

Correct! "Run out of" means to have no more of something.

9. I need to ________ what time the train leaves.

find out
find up
find off

Correct! "Find out" means to discover or learn information.

10. She was offered the job but she ________ it ________.

turned... on
turned... down
turned... off

Correct! "Turn down" means to reject or refuse an offer.

Practice: Reported Speech

INSTRUCCIONES

Transforma las oraciones a Reported Speech o elige la opcion correcta.

Exercise 1: Reported Statements

1. "I am very happy." - She said...

she is very happy
she was very happy
I was very happy

Correct! Present Simple → Past Simple, and "I" → "she".

2. "I will call you tomorrow." - He told me...

he will call me tomorrow
he would call you the next day
he would call me the next day

Correct! Will → would, "you" → "me", "tomorrow" → "the next day".

3. "I have finished my homework." - She said...

she had finished her homework
she has finished her homework
she had finished my homework

Correct! Present Perfect → Past Perfect, "I" → "she", "my" → "her".

Exercise 2: SAY vs TELL

4. He ________ me that he was leaving.

said
told
said to

Correct! TELL + person (me). "Said me" is WRONG.

5. She ________ that the meeting was cancelled.

said
told
told that

Correct! SAY + that (no person). TELL needs a person object.

Exercise 3: Reported Questions

6. "Where do you live?" - He asked me...

where did I live
where I lived
where do I live

Correct! No inversion in reported questions: "where I lived" (not "where did I live").

7. "Are you coming to the party?" - She asked...

that I was coming to the party
was I coming to the party
if I was coming to the party

Correct! Yes/No questions use IF or WHETHER, no inversion.

Exercise 4: Reported Commands and Requests

8. "Please close the window." - She asked me...

to close the window
that I close the window
close the window

Correct! Requests: asked + person + TO + infinitive.

9. "Don't touch that!" - He told the children...

to not touch that
not to touch that
don't touch that

Correct! Negative commands: NOT TO + infinitive (not "to not").

Exercise 5: Reporting Verbs

10. "I didn't break the vase." - He ________ breaking the vase.

refused
denied
admitted

Correct! DENY + -ing = negar haber hecho algo. "He denied breaking the vase."

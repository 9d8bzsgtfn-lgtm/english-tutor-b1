Vocabulary: Linking Words & Connectors

TEMA

Vocabulario de conectores organizados por funcion, con expresiones formales e informales para escribir essays y textos coherentes.

/aend/

Cause Connectors - Conectores de causa

because
/bi'koz/

/bi'koz ov/

"The match was cancelled because of the rain."

/sins/

"Since you're here, let's start the meeting."

"As it was getting late, we decided to leave."

Result Connectors - Conectores de consecuencia

so
/sou/
asi que, por eso
"It was late, so we took a taxi."
therefore
/'therfor/
por lo tanto (formal)
"He didn't study. Therefore, he failed the exam."
as a result
/aez e ri'zelt/
como resultado
"He exercised daily. As a result, he lost weight."
consequently
/'konsikwentli/
por consiguiente (formal)
"Prices rose. Consequently, sales dropped."
that's why
/thaets wai/
por eso (informal)
"I love languages. That's why I became a teacher."
for this reason
/for this 'ri:zen/
por esta razon
"For this reason, I chose this university."

Example Connectors - Conectores de ejemplo

/for ig'za:mpel/

/for 'instens/

/sech aez/

like

Formal vs Informal Expressions

REGISTRO - FORMAL VS INFORMAL
Funcion Informal (speaking) Formal (writing/essays)
Adicion and, also, too, as well moreover, furthermore, in addition
Contraste but, though however, nevertheless, on the other hand
Causa because since, as, due to, owing to
Consecuencia so, that's why therefore, consequently, as a result
Ejemplo like for example, for instance, such as

FRASES PARA ESSAYS B1

Grammar: Linking Words & Connectors

IMPRESCINDIBLE - MEMORIZAR

¿Qué son los conectores?

Los linking words o connectors son palabras y expresiones que unen ideas dentro de una frase o entre frases. Son fundamentales para escribir y hablar con fluidez a nivel B1.

Función Conectores principales Ejemplo
Adición and, also, moreover I speak English and French.
Contraste but, however, although It was raining, but we went out.
Causa because, since, due to I stayed home because I was ill.
Consecuencia so, therefore, as a result It was late, so we took a taxi.

1. Conectores de Adición (Addition)

ADDING INFORMATION
Conector Registro Posición Ejemplo
and Neutro Entre elementos She's smart and hardworking.
also Neutro Antes del verbo principal / después de BE He also speaks German.
too Informal Final de frase I love pizza too.
as well Informal Final de frase She plays tennis as well.
moreover Formal Inicio de frase + coma Moreover, the price is reasonable.
furthermore Formal Inicio de frase + coma Furthermore, it's easy to use.
in addition Formal Inicio de frase + coma In addition, we offer free delivery.
besides Semi-formal Inicio de frase + coma Besides, I don't have time.

2. Conectores de Contraste (Contrast)

CONTRASTING IDEAS - MUY FRECUENTE EN B1
Conector Estructura Ejemplo
but ..., but + frase I tried hard, but I failed.
however Frase. However, + frase It was expensive. However, I bought it.
although / though Although + frase, frase Although it was cold, we went swimming.
even though Even though + frase, frase Even though I'm tired, I'll help you.
despite Despite + nombre/-ing Despite the rain, we had fun.
in spite of In spite of + nombre/-ing In spite of being tired, she kept working.
nevertheless Frase. Nevertheless, + frase It's risky. Nevertheless, I'll try.
on the other hand Frase. On the other hand, + frase It's cheap. On the other hand, the quality is poor.
ALTHOUGH vs DESPITE - DIFERENCIA CLAVE
Conector Va seguido de... Ejemplo
Although / Even though Sujeto + verbo (frase completa) Although it was raining, we went out.
Despite / In spite of Nombre o -ing (NO frase completa) Despite the rain, we went out.

Truco: Despite/In spite of + the fact that + frase completa

Despite the fact that it was raining, we went out.

3. Conectores de Causa (Cause/Reason)

GIVING REASONS
Conector Estructura Ejemplo
because ...because + frase I left early because I was tired.
because of ...because of + nombre/-ing The match was cancelled because of the rain.
since Since + frase, frase Since you're here, can you help me?
as As + frase, frase As it was late, we decided to leave.
due to ...due to + nombre The delay was due to technical problems.
owing to ...owing to + nombre Owing to the weather, the event was postponed.
BECAUSE vs BECAUSE OF - DIFERENCIA CLAVE
Conector Va seguido de... Ejemplo
Because Sujeto + verbo (frase completa) I stayed home because I was ill.
Because of Nombre o -ing (NO frase) I stayed home because of my illness.

4. Conectores de Consecuencia (Result)

SHOWING RESULTS
Conector Registro Posición Ejemplo
so Neutro ..., so + frase It was raining, so I took an umbrella.
therefore Formal Frase. Therefore, + frase He didn't study. Therefore, he failed.
as a result Formal Frase. As a result, + frase The roads were icy. As a result, there were many accidents.
consequently Formal Frase. Consequently, + frase He was late. Consequently, he missed the bus.
that's why Informal Frase. That's why + frase I was hungry. That's why I ate so much.
for this reason Formal Frase. For this reason, + frase It's cheaper. For this reason, I chose it.

5. Conectores de Secuencia (Sequence)

ORDERING IDEAS
Para empezar Para continuar Para terminar
First / Firstly Then Finally
First of all Next Lastly
To begin with After that In the end
To start with Afterwards Eventually
In the first place Second / Secondly To conclude

Ejemplo completo:

First, preheat the oven. Then, mix the ingredients. After that, pour the mixture into a pan. Finally, bake for 30 minutes.

6. Conectores de Ejemplo (Giving Examples)

EJEMPLIFICAR
Conector Estructura Ejemplo
for example ..., for example, ... I love fruit, for example, apples and oranges.
for instance ..., for instance, ... Some countries, for instance Japan, have low crime rates.
such as ... such as ... Sports such as tennis and swimming are popular.
like ... like ... Cities like London and Paris are expensive.
e.g. (escrito) ... e.g. ... Bring formal clothes, e.g. a suit or dress.

7. Otros conectores importantes

CONECTORES ADICIONALES
Función Conector Ejemplo
Condición if, unless, as long as If you study, you'll pass.
Propósito to, in order to, so that I'm saving money to buy a car.
Comparación similarly, likewise, in the same way Similarly, Japan has strict rules.
Conclusión in conclusion, to sum up, in summary In conclusion, technology has both advantages and disadvantages.
Opinión in my opinion, I believe, personally In my opinion, this is the best solution.

Posición de los conectores en la frase

POSICIÓN CORRECTA
Posición Conectores Puntuación
Inicio de frase However, Therefore, Moreover, Furthermore, Nevertheless Conector, + frase
Medio de frase and, but, so, because, although Frase, + conector + frase
Final de frase too, as well, though Frase + conector

Errores comunes a evitar

ERRORES FRECUENTES
Incorrecto Correcto Por qué
Although it was raining, but we went out. Although it was raining, we went out. ALTHOUGH ya indica contraste, no se añade BUT
Despite it was cold... Despite the cold... / Despite being cold... DESPITE + nombre/-ing, NO frase completa
Because of I was tired... Because I was tired... / Because of my tiredness... BECAUSE + frase, BECAUSE OF + nombre
I like coffee. However I prefer tea. I like coffee. However, I prefer tea. HOWEVER al inicio lleva coma después
I want for to learn English. I want to learn English. TO (no FOR TO) para expresar propósito
Such as London, Paris... Cities such as London, Paris... SUCH AS necesita un sustantivo antes
RESUMEN "SUBE LAS ANTENAS"
  • Adición: and, also, moreover, furthermore, in addition
  • Contraste: but, however, although, despite, in spite of
  • Causa: because (+ frase), because of (+ nombre), since, due to
  • Consecuencia: so, therefore, as a result, consequently
  • Secuencia: first, then, after that, finally
  • Ejemplo: for example, such as, for instance
  • Recuerda: Although/Because + frase vs Despite/Because of + nombre

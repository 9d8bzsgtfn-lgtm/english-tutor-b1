Practice: Linking Words & Connectors

INSTRUCCIONES

Selecciona el conector correcto para cada frase.

Exercise 1: Choose the correct connector

1. I wanted to buy the dress, ________ it was too expensive.

but
so
because

Correct! "But" shows contrast between wanting to buy and not being able to afford it.

2. She studied hard. ________, she passed all her exams.

However
Therefore
Although

Correct! "Therefore" shows result - studying hard led to passing exams.

3. ________ the bad weather, we decided to go hiking.

Although
Despite
Because of

Correct! "Despite" + noun phrase shows contrast. "Although" would need a full clause (Although the weather was bad).

4. The restaurant was expensive. ________, the food was delicious.

However
Therefore
Moreover

Correct! "However" shows contrast between the price and the quality.

5. I couldn't sleep ________ I drank too much coffee.

so
because
but

Correct! "Because" introduces the reason why you couldn't sleep.

Exercise 2: Although vs Despite

6. ________ she was very tired, she finished all her work.

Although
Despite
Because

Correct! "Although" is followed by a subject + verb (she was). "Despite" would need a noun (Despite her tiredness).

7. ________ being rich, he lives a simple life.

Although
Despite
However

Correct! "Despite" is followed by -ing or a noun. "Although" would need "Although he is rich".

Exercise 3: Cause and Result

8. The flight was cancelled ________ the fog.

because
due to
so

Correct! "Due to" is followed by a noun (the fog). "Because" would need a full clause (because there was fog).

9. It was raining heavily, ________ we decided to stay at home.

because
so
although

Correct! "So" introduces the result of the heavy rain.

10. ________, I'd like to thank everyone for coming. ________, let's start the presentation.

First / Now
However / Therefore
Although / But

Correct! "First" and "Now" are sequence connectors used to order ideas.

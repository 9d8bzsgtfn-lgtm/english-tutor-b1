# Common Errors for Spanish Speakers Learning English

Este documento recopila los errores más frecuentes que cometen los hispanohablantes al aprender inglés, con explicaciones claras y ejemplos de cómo corregirlos.

---

## 1. False Friends (Falsos Amigos)

Palabras que parecen similares pero tienen significados diferentes.

| Spanish | Parece... | Realmente significa | En inglés es |
|---------|-----------|---------------------|--------------|
| actual | actual | current, present | real, true |
| actualmente | actually | currently, nowadays | actually |
| asistir | assist | attend | help, assist |
| atender | attend | serve, help | pay attention |
| avisar | advise | warn, inform | recommend |
| bizarro | bizarre | brave, generous | strange, weird |
| campo | camp | field, countryside | camp |
| carpeta | carpet | folder | alfombra |
| casualidad | casualty | coincidence | víctima |
| collar | collar | necklace | cuello (camisa) |
| compromiso | compromise | commitment | acuerdo mutuo |
| conductor | conductor | driver | director (orquesta) |
| constipado | constipated | have a cold | estreñido |
| decepción | deception | disappointment | engaño |
| disgusto | disgust | annoyance, upset | asco |
| embarazada | embarrassed | pregnant | avergonzado |
| éxito | exit | success | salida |
| fábrica | fabric | factory | tela |
| grosería | grocery | rudeness | comestibles |
| introducir | introduce | insert, enter | presentar |
| largo | large | long | grande |
| librería | library | bookshop | biblioteca |
| molestar | molest | bother, annoy | abusar |
| noticia | notice | news | aviso |
| pariente | parent | relative | padre/madre |
| pretender | pretend | intend, try | fingir |
| realizar | realize | carry out, make | darse cuenta |
| recordar | record | remember | grabar |
| sano | sane | healthy | cuerdo |
| sensible | sensible | sensitive | sensato |
| simpático | sympathetic | nice, friendly | comprensivo |
| soportar | support | tolerate, stand | apoyar |
| suceso | success | event | éxito |
| últimamente | ultimately | lately, recently | finalmente |

### Ejemplos de errores y correcciones

| Incorrecto | Correcto | Explicación |
|------------|----------|-------------|
| ~~I have an important actual problem.~~ | I have an important **current** problem. | "actual" = real |
| ~~Actually I live in Madrid.~~ | **Currently** I live in Madrid. | "actually" = en realidad |
| ~~I am very sensible.~~ | I am very **sensitive**. | "sensible" = sensato |
| ~~She is embarrassed.~~ (quiere decir embarazada) | She is **pregnant**. | "embarrassed" = avergonzada |
| ~~I don't support this noise.~~ | I can't **stand** this noise. | "support" = apoyar |

## 2. Grammar Errors from Spanish Interference

### 2.1 Subject Pronouns (Omisión del sujeto)

En español se puede omitir el sujeto, pero en inglés es **obligatorio**.

| Incorrecto | Correcto |
|------------|----------|
| ~~Is raining.~~ | **It** is raining. |
| ~~Am tired.~~ | **I** am tired. |
| ~~Is important to study.~~ | **It** is important to study. |
| ~~Are five people here.~~ | **There** are five people here. |

---

### 2.2 Verb "To Be" vs "To Have"

En español usamos "tener" donde en inglés se usa "to be".

| Español | Incorrecto | Correcto |
|---------|------------|----------|
| Tengo 25 años | ~~I have 25 years.~~ | I **am** 25 (years old). |
| Tengo hambre | ~~I have hunger.~~ | I **am** hungry. |
| Tengo sed | ~~I have thirst.~~ | I **am** thirsty. |
| Tengo frío | ~~I have cold.~~ | I **am** cold. |
| Tengo calor | ~~I have hot.~~ | I **am** hot. |
| Tengo sueño | ~~I have sleep.~~ | I **am** sleepy. |
| Tengo miedo | ~~I have fear.~~ | I **am** afraid. |
| Tengo razón | ~~I have reason.~~ | I **am** right. |
| Tengo suerte | ~~I have luck.~~ | I **am** lucky. |
| Tengo prisa | ~~I have hurry.~~ | I **am** in a hurry. |

---

### 2.3 "To Be" vs "To Have" (Experiencias)

| Español | Incorrecto | Correcto |
|---------|------------|----------|
| ¿Has estado en París? | ~~Have you been in Paris?~~ | Have you **been to** Paris? |
| Nunca he estado en Japón | ~~I've never been in Japan.~~ | I've never **been to** Japan. |

---

### 2.4 "Agree" sin "To Be"

En español: "Estoy de acuerdo" (con verbo estar)
En inglés: "I agree" (sin verbo to be)

| Incorrecto | Correcto |
|------------|----------|
| ~~I am agree.~~ | I **agree**. |
| ~~I am not agree.~~ | I **don't agree** / I **disagree**. |
| ~~Are you agree?~~ | Do you **agree**? |

---

### 2.5 Double Negatives

En español se permiten dobles negativos; en inglés formal, no.

| Español | Incorrecto | Correcto |
|---------|------------|----------|
| No tengo nada | ~~I don't have nothing.~~ | I don't have **anything**. / I have **nothing**. |
| No veo a nadie | ~~I don't see nobody.~~ | I don't see **anybody**. / I see **nobody**. |
| Nunca voy a ningún sitio | ~~I never go nowhere.~~ | I never go **anywhere**. / I go **nowhere**. |

---

### 2.6 Word Order: Adjectives Before Nouns

En español, el adjetivo suele ir después del sustantivo. En inglés, **antes**.

| Español | Incorrecto | Correcto |
|---------|------------|----------|
| Casa grande | ~~house big~~ | **big** house |
| Coche rojo | ~~car red~~ | **red** car |
| Día hermoso | ~~day beautiful~~ | **beautiful** day |

---

### 2.7 Adverb Placement

Los adverbios de frecuencia van **antes del verbo principal** (o después de "be").

| Incorrecto | Correcto |
|------------|----------|
| ~~I go always to the gym.~~ | I **always go** to the gym. |
| ~~She drinks never coffee.~~ | She **never drinks** coffee. |
| ~~I am late always.~~ | I am **always** late. |

---

### 2.8 Question Formation

En español cambiamos entonación; en inglés, necesitamos auxiliares e inversión.

| Incorrecto | Correcto |
|------------|----------|
| ~~You like coffee?~~ | **Do** you like coffee? |
| ~~She is coming?~~ | **Is** she coming? |
| ~~He works here?~~ | **Does** he work here? |
| ~~Where you live?~~ | Where **do** you live? |
| ~~What means this?~~ | What **does** this mean? |

---

### 2.9 Articles

#### Omisión del artículo indefinido con profesiones

| Incorrecto | Correcto |
|------------|----------|
| ~~I am teacher.~~ | I am **a** teacher. |
| ~~She is doctor.~~ | She is **a** doctor. |

#### Uso del artículo definido donde no corresponde

| Incorrecto | Correcto | Explicación |
|------------|----------|-------------|
| ~~The life is beautiful.~~ | **Life** is beautiful. | Conceptos generales |
| ~~I like the music.~~ | I like **music**. | En general |
| ~~The dogs are loyal.~~ | **Dogs** are loyal. | En general |
| ~~I study the English.~~ | I study **English**. | Idiomas |
| ~~The Spain is beautiful.~~ | **Spain** is beautiful. | Países (excepciones: the UK, the USA) |

---

### 2.10 Prepositions

#### "In" vs "On" vs "At"

| Contexto | Preposición | Ejemplo |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| Países, ciudades | **in** | I live **in** Madrid. |
| Calles | **in** (UK) / **on** (US) | I live **in/on** Oak Street. |
| Direcciones específicas | **at** | I live **at** 25 Oak Street. |
| Meses, años, estaciones | **in** | **In** January, **in** 2024, **in** summer |
| Días, fechas | **on** | **On** Monday, **on** July 4th |
| Horas | **at** | **At** 5 o'clock, **at** noon |

| Incorrecto | Correcto |
|------------|----------|
| ~~I was born in 1990.~~ | I was born **in** 1990. ✓ |
| ~~In Monday I have a meeting.~~ | **On** Monday I have a meeting. |
| ~~I wake up in 7am.~~ | I wake up **at** 7am. |

#### Preposiciones específicas

| Español | Incorrecto | Correcto |
|---------|------------|----------|
| Depende de | ~~depends of~~ | depends **on** |
| Consiste en | ~~consists in~~ | consists **of** |
| Casarse con | ~~marry with~~ | marry (someone) / get married **to** |
| Soñar con | ~~dream with~~ | dream **of/about** |
| Pensar en | ~~think in~~ | think **about/of** |
| Esperar a | ~~wait~~ (sin prep.) | wait **for** |
| Escuchar a | ~~listen~~ (sin prep.) | listen **to** |
| Mirar a | ~~look~~ (sin prep.) | look **at** |
| Buscar | ~~search~~ (sin prep.) | look **for** / search **for** |
| Pedir | ~~ask~~ (sin prep.) | ask **for** |
| Entrar en | ~~enter in~~ | enter (sin prep.) |
| Llegar a | ~~arrive to~~ | arrive **at/in** |

### 2.11 Present Perfect vs Past Simple

Hispanohablantes tienden a usar Past Simple donde corresponde Present Perfect.

| Contexto | Incorrecto | Correcto |
|----------|------------|----------|
| Experiencia (sin tiempo específico) | ~~Did you ever go to London?~~ | **Have** you ever **been** to London? |
| Acción reciente con relevancia actual | ~~I lost my keys.~~ (si todavía las busco) | I**'ve lost** my keys. |
| Con "just", "already", "yet" | ~~I just finished.~~ (en UK) | I**'ve just finished**. |

**Regla:** Si mencionas CUÁNDO, usa Past Simple. Si no, usa Present Perfect.

- I **went** to Paris **last year**. ✓
- I **have been** to Paris. ✓ (en algún momento de mi vida)

### 2.12 "Make" vs "Do"

| Incorrecto | Correcto |
|------------|----------|
| ~~make homework~~ | **do** homework |
| ~~make exercise~~ | **do** exercise |
| ~~do a mistake~~ | **make** a mistake |
| ~~do a decision~~ | **make** a decision |
| ~~do a photo~~ | **take** a photo |
| ~~do a question~~ | **ask** a question |

**Regla general:**
- **Make:** crear algo nuevo, producir (make a cake, make coffee, make a decision)
- **Do:** actividades, tareas (do homework, do the dishes, do exercise)

---

### 2.13 Confusión "Say" vs "Tell"

| Verbo | Uso | Ejemplo |
|-------|-----|---------|
| **say** | say something | She **said** "hello". |
| **say** | say (that)... | He **said** that he was tired. |
| **tell** | tell someone | She **told me** the truth. |
| **tell** | tell someone (that)... | He **told me** that he was tired. |

| Incorrecto | Correcto |
|------------|----------|
| ~~She said me the truth.~~ | She **told** me the truth. |
| ~~He told that he was tired.~~ | He **said** that he was tired. |

---

### 2.14 Confusión "Borrow" vs "Lend"

| Verbo | Significado | Dirección |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| **borrow** | pedir prestado | hacia ti |
| **lend** | prestar | desde ti |

| Incorrecto | Correcto |
|------------|----------|
| ~~Can you borrow me your pen?~~ | Can you **lend** me your pen? |
| ~~Can I lend your book?~~ | Can I **borrow** your book? |

---

### 2.15 "Another" vs "Other"

| Palabra | Uso |
|---------|-----|
| **another** | + singular (one more) |
| **other** | + plural o con "the" |
| **others** | pronombre plural |

| Incorrecto | Correcto |
|------------|----------|
| ~~I want other coffee.~~ | I want **another** coffee. |
| ~~I need another informations.~~ | I need **other/more** information. |
| ~~The anothers are here.~~ | **The others** are here. |

## 3. Pronunciation Challenges

### 3.1 Sonidos difíciles para hispanohablantes

| Sonido | Ejemplo | Error común | Consejo |
|--------|---------|-------------|---------|
| /v/ | very, video | pronunciar como /b/ | Muerde el labio inferior |
| /z/ | zoo, zero | pronunciar como /s/ | Vibra las cuerdas vocales |
| /ʃ/ vs /tʃ/ | she vs cheese | confundirlos | /ʃ/ es continuo, /tʃ/ es explosivo |
| /dʒ/ | job, just | pronunciar como /y/ | Como la "j" en "John" |
| /θ/ | think, both | pronunciar como /t/ o /s/ | Lengua entre los dientes |
| /ð/ | this, that | pronunciar como /d/ | Lengua entre los dientes (sonoro) |
| /h/ | house, hello | no pronunciar | Aspirar aire |
| /ŋ/ | singing | pronunciar /ng/ | Nasal sin /g/ |
| Schwa /ə/ | about, problem | sobrearticular | Sonido neutro, relajado |

## 4. Common Vocabulary Mistakes

### 4.1 "People" es plural

| Incorrecto | Correcto |
|------------|----------|
| ~~People is...~~ | People **are**... |
| ~~A people~~ | **A person** |
| ~~Many persons~~ | Many **people** |

### 4.2 "Information", "Advice", "News" son incontables

| Incorrecto | Correcto |
|------------|----------|
| ~~informations~~ | information / pieces of information |
| ~~advices~~ | advice / pieces of advice |
| ~~a news~~ | a piece of news / some news |
| ~~The news are good.~~ | The news **is** good. |

### 4.3 "Fun" vs "Funny"

| Palabra | Significado | Ejemplo |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **fun** | divertido (experiencia) | The party was **fun**. |
| **funny** | gracioso (que hace reír) | The comedian is **funny**. |

| Incorrecto | Correcto |
|------------|----------|
| ~~The movie was very funny.~~ (si no era de humor) | The movie was **fun** / **entertaining**. |

### 4.4 "History" vs "Story"

| Palabra | Significado |
|---------|-------------|
| **history** | historia (eventos pasados, asignatura) |
| **story** | historia (cuento, relato) |

| Incorrecto | Correcto |
|------------|----------|
| ~~Tell me a history.~~ | Tell me a **story**. |
| ~~The history of Rome~~ | The **history** of Rome. ✓ |

### 4.5 "Assist" vs "Attend"

| Palabra | Significado |
|---------|-------------|
| **assist** | ayudar |
| **attend** | asistir (ir a un evento) |

| Incorrecto | Correcto |
|------------|----------|
| ~~I will assist the meeting.~~ | I will **attend** the meeting. |

### 4.6 "Actually" vs "Currently"

| Palabra | Significado |
|---------|-------------|
| **actually** | en realidad, de hecho |
| **currently** | actualmente |

| Incorrecto | Correcto |
|------------|----------|
| ~~Actually I live in Madrid.~~ | **Currently** I live in Madrid. |
| ~~I actually work there.~~ (para decir actualmente) | I **currently** work there. |

## 5. Spelling Errors

### 5.1 Confusión entre palabras similares

| Error | Correcto | Significado |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| ~~there~~ (posesivo) | **their** | su/sus (de ellos) |
| ~~their~~ (lugar) | **there** | allí |
| ~~they're~~ (mal usado) | **they're** = they are | ellos son/están |
| ~~your~~ (eres) | **you're** = you are | tú eres |
| ~~its~~ (es) | **it's** = it is | es/está |
| ~~it's~~ (posesivo) | **its** | su (de ello) |
| ~~to~~ (también) | **too** | también, demasiado |
| ~~then~~ (que) | **than** | que (comparativo) |
| ~~were~~ (dónde) | **where** | dónde |
| ~~lose~~ (suelto) | **loose** | suelto, flojo |
| ~~loose~~ (perder) | **lose** | perder |

## 6. Quick Reference: Top 20 Errors to Avoid

1. ~~I am agree~~ → I agree
2. ~~I have 30 years~~ → I am 30 years old
3. ~~I am interesting in...~~ → I am interested in...
4. ~~People is~~ → People are
5. ~~I am very tired~~ → I am very tired ✓ (pero ~~I have very tired~~ es incorrecto)
6. ~~Depends of~~ → Depends on
7. ~~I don't have nothing~~ → I don't have anything
8. ~~Is raining~~ → It is raining
9. ~~House big~~ → Big house
10. ~~Did you ever go?~~ → Have you ever been?
11. ~~She said me~~ → She told me
12. ~~I go always~~ → I always go
13. ~~Actually~~ (queriendo decir actualmente) → Currently
14. ~~Do a mistake~~ → Make a mistake
15. ~~Wait him~~ → Wait for him
16. ~~Listen me~~ → Listen to me
17. ~~Arrive to~~ → Arrive at/in
18. ~~Marry with~~ → Marry / Get married to
19. ~~The English~~ → English (idiomas sin artículo)
20. ~~The life is~~ → Life is (conceptos generales sin artículo)
